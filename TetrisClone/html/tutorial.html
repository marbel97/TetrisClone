Controls

Press the Left and Right arrow keys to move the piece

Press the Up arrow key to rotate the piece clockwise

Press the Down arrow key to speed up the piece

Scoring points

Completely filling a row will erase it and all the rows above will fall by one row each.
Also you can clear multiple rows at the same time and doing that will provide bonus points.
To maximize your score always make sure to clear as many rows at a time as you can!

Game Over

The game ends when a piece tries to settle at least partially outside of the screen so be careful not to stack too many pieces without clearing rows!
